CROP YEILD PREDICTION

Crop yield prediction is the process of estimating the possible harvest of a given crop by utilizing cutting-edge technologies including machine learning algorithms, weather data, and satellite photography. Farmers and agricultural specialists can make well-informed judgments about planting schedules, resource allocation, and risk management by examining past data and present environmental circumstances. Precise forecasts of production enable farmers to enhance their methods, better manage resources, and adjust to shifting weather patterns.
