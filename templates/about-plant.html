PLANT DISEASE DETECTION

Plant disease prediction is a crucial aspect of modern agriculture that uses cutting-edge technologies to foresee and manage potential dangers to crop health. In order to create predictive models, data from several sources are combined, such as historical disease incidence, soil properties, and weather trends. These models forecast the likelihood of specific plant diseases in a given area using data analytics and machine learning.
